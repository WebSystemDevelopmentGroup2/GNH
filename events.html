Events

Check back here for new and recent Gamma Nu Eta events!

Company Exposé

The Company Exposé is organized by Gamma Nu Eta and it is the largest ITWS recruitment event. It features students and recruiters engaging in casual games and activities. Additional details including the companies attending and location of the event are typically announced in early September.

Newsletter

The newsletter is an annual mailing that Gamma Nu Eta sends to alumni updating them about the events in the ITWS department and the institution at large.

Tutoring

Gamma Nu Eta holds weekly tutoring sessions every Wednesday. At these tutoring sessions, GNH can help you with any course in your ITWS degree program. This includes ITWS core classes, such as Intro to ITWS and Web Systems, or classes outside of ITWS, such as Computer Science 1 and Data Structures. We can also help with any questions that you might have about career development, resumes, as well as just general questions about RPI.
